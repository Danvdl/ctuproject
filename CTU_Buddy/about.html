About

CTU-Buddy

CTU-Buddy is designed to be an online educational system or tool that can be used by students of CTU college to further help them throughout their college career. Its purpose is to be a comprehensive and interactive website that helps the learning experience for students and helps them to do the best they possibly can while at CTU college. It is designed by CTU students to help CTU students. It designed to be used by all students around south africa, to help getting resources and all possible help for those in diffucult situations.

CTU Footprint

CTU Training Solutions is an award-winning private tertiary education provider and has been developing skilled professionals since 1987. We offer training solutions for full time and part time students. Our national qualifications and international vendor-related certifications are updated regularly according to industry standards and pave the road to success for our graduates entering future jobs introduced by the 4th Industrial Revolution.

We also offer short courses that are skills-based and vendor-specific. International certifications are available to specific relevant qualifications. CTU Training Solutions is a Level 2 B-BBEE contributor and can assist companies with learnerships. CTU is also registered with the Department of Higher Education as a Private Higher Education Institution. We have over 14 registered ICT learnerships up to NQF Level 6.

With faculties in IT, Design, Business & Management, Engineering and Humanities, participants have the choice to study full time (1 to 3-year qualifications and technical and other diplomas) or part time (qualifications, short courses and skills development programmes).
